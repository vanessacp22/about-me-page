Meet Karen

This is Karen — my xArm 1S robotic arm and the first official member of my future lab. She’s temperamental, precise, and endlessly curious — just like any good collaborator. I’m using Karen to dive deeper into structured machine learning, automation, and embedded systems, exploring how robotics can bridge creativity and utility. What started as a test bench project has become a small-scale ecosystem in progress — a space to prototype, experiment, and eventually teach others how accessible robotics can be.
